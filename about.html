Taylor Cole is a 22-year-old photographer based in Peoria, IL. She believes that there is always something new to see, and her photography allows her not to simply look, but to truly see the world. Through her photographs, she encourages the viewer to pause and discover beauty in the fleeting moments that often go unnoticed. When she isn't taking photographs, Taylor spends her time reading everything in sight, exploring abandoned buildings, writing poetry, and watching weird indie films.
